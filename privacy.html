Saturdays

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026
Saturdays is built to be private by design. Everything you enter stays on your device. We don't run accounts, servers, analytics, ads, or trackers. We never see your data — because it never leaves your phone.

What we collect

Nothing. Saturdays does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There is no sign-up and no account.

What you enter, and where it lives

To show your Saturday count, the app stores the details you type — your birth date, sex (for the life-expectancy estimate), optional health toggles, the people you add, and your weekly presence check-ins — only on your device, using your device's local storage. This data:

  • is never sent to us or to any third party;
  • is not backed up to any server we control;
  • can be erased at any time by deleting the app or clearing the app's data.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Saturdays schedules a weekly reminder locally on your device. These are device-local notifications — no push server is involved and no information is sent anywhere.

Health data

Saturdays does not read from or write to Apple Health, and does not access any health records. The life-expectancy estimate is calculated on your device from public U.S. actuarial (SSA period) life tables. It is a population-level estimate for reflection only — not a medical prediction, diagnosis, or advice.

Sharing an image

When you tap Share, the app creates an image on your device and hands it to the system share sheet. You choose where it goes (Messages, a social app, your photo library, etc.). The app does not upload the image anywhere on its own.

Third parties

Saturdays includes no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The web version is served as static files; the app runs entirely on-device.

Children

Saturdays is not directed to children and is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone — because we do not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
