Privacy Policy

Effective June 25, 2026 · Last updated June 25, 2026
Saturdays is built to be private by design. Everything you enter stays on your device. We don't run accounts, servers, analytics, ads, or trackers. We never see your data — because it never leaves your phone.

Introduction

Welcome to Saturdays ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"). Saturdays is a mortality-awareness and wellness application that helps you reflect on the time you have by estimating remaining Saturdays based on life-expectancy data and tracking daily health habits.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Saturdays iOS application. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

When using the App, you may voluntarily provide the following:

Personal profile data: your date of birth, biological sex (used solely for actuarial life-expectancy calculations), country of residence, and display name or nickname.

Health and lifestyle data: information related to your health habits and lifestyle factors, including but not limited to body mass index (BMI) or the height and weight used to calculate it, exercise frequency and type, smoking status, alcohol consumption habits, sleep duration and quality, and other wellness-related inputs you choose to provide.

Family member data: if you use the App's family and relationship features, you may enter limited information about other people, such as names or nicknames of family members (e.g., parents, children, spouse, or partner), their dates of birth, their relationship to you, and their biological sex (for life-expectancy estimates).

1.2 Information we do not collect

We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. All data entered into Saturdays is stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not create user accounts or require registration.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry services of any kind.
  • We do not use cookies, advertising identifiers, or fingerprinting technologies.
  • We do not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device sensors beyond what is necessary for the App's stated features.
  • We do not integrate with Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, or any third-party health-data platform.
  • We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third party for any purpose.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

2.1 Local-only storage

All data you enter into Saturdays is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage mechanisms (such as UserDefaults, Core Data, or on-device databases). Your data never leaves your device through the App.

2.2 Device security

Your data is protected by the security features built into your iOS device, including device passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, and iOS data encryption at rest. We recommend keeping your device's security features enabled to protect your data.

2.3 Backups

If you use iCloud Backup or iTunes/Finder backup for your device, your Saturdays data may be included in that backup. These backups are governed by Apple's privacy policy and your own backup settings, not by this Privacy Policy. You can manage your backup preferences through your device's Settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • Life-expectancy estimates: to calculate an estimated number of remaining Saturdays based on actuarial life-expectancy tables, adjusted by the health and lifestyle factors you provide.
  • Health-habit tracking: to display your daily health-habit data back to you within the App for your own personal reflection and wellness awareness.
  • Family features: to show you time-based relationship visualizations with family members you choose to add.
  • Shareable images: if you use the App's sharing feature, to generate a visual "reveal" image that you can choose to share via your device's native sharing capabilities.

We do not use your data for any purpose other than providing the App's core functionality as described above.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 No third-party sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third party. Period. Because all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to it and no ability to share it.

4.2 User-initiated sharing

If you choose to use the App's sharing feature to share a reveal image, that sharing is performed through your device's native share sheet (e.g., Messages, email, social media). Once you share content through these channels, that content is governed by the privacy policies of the respective platforms you share to. We encourage you to review those policies.

4.3 Legal requirements

Because we do not collect or have access to your data, we cannot respond to legal requests for user data. Your data exists only on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Current third-party integrations

As of the effective date of this Privacy Policy, Saturdays does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party service, SDK, API, analytics platform, advertising network, or cloud service.

5.2 Future changes

If we ever introduce third-party services in a future version of the App, we will update this Privacy Policy before those changes take effect, clearly disclose what data would be shared and with whom, and, where required by law, obtain your affirmative consent before any data is transmitted to third parties. We will notify you of material changes through the App or through the App Store update notes.

6. Children's Privacy

6.1 Age restriction

Saturdays is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App's content involves mortality awareness and life-expectancy calculations, which may not be appropriate for young children.

6.2 COPPA compliance

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA): we do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under 13. Because the App does not transmit data from the device and does not create accounts, no child data is collected by us. If a child under 13 uses the App on their own device, all data remains local to that device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used the App, you can delete all App data by uninstalling the App or using the in-app data-deletion feature described in Section 8.

6.3 Parental guidance

We recommend that parents and guardians review the App's content before allowing minors between 13 and 17 to use it. The App's themes of mortality and life expectancy may benefit from parental context and conversation.

7. Health Data

7.1 Nature of health data

The App stores health-related information that you voluntarily provide — including lifestyle factors such as exercise habits, smoking and drinking status, sleep patterns, and BMI-related data — locally on your device. We do not collect, receive, or transmit it. This data is used solely, on-device, to adjust life-expectancy estimates and is not diagnostic, clinical, or medical in nature.

7.2 Not medical data

The health-related data you enter into Saturdays is self-reported lifestyle information. It is not medical data, clinical health records, or protected health information (PHI) as defined under HIPAA. The App does not access Apple HealthKit, electronic health records, or any clinical data source.

7.3 No health-data sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your health-related data to any third party, including insurers, employers, data brokers, or advertisers. Your health-related data never leaves your device through the App.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

8.1 Data retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you have the App installed and choose to keep your data. We do not retain any copy of your data on any server or external system.

8.2 Deleting your data

You can delete all of your Saturdays data at any time by using the "Delete All Data" option within the App's Settings screen, which permanently erases all data stored by the App on your device, or by uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored App data.

8.3 Irrecoverability

Because your data is stored only on your device and we do not maintain any backup or copy, deleted data cannot be recovered by us. Please be sure you want to delete your data before doing so.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 California (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act:

  • Right to know: this Privacy Policy provides that disclosure. Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have direct access to all of your data within the App at all times.
  • Right to delete: you can delete all of your data at any time using the methods in Section 8.
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing: we do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to non-discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising any privacy right.
  • Global Privacy Control: we do not track users across websites or apps, so GPC signals are not applicable. We honor the spirit of such signals by not engaging in any tracking.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the App's functionality.

9.2 European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access: you can access all of your data directly within the App at any time.
  • Right to rectification: you can edit or correct any data you have entered within the App at any time.
  • Right to erasure: you can delete all of your data using the methods in Section 8.
  • Right to restriction of processing: because data is processed locally on your device and we have no access to it, you control all processing.
  • Right to data portability: because your data is stored locally, you have full control over it on your device. The App does not currently offer a data-export feature, but you may request one by contacting us.
  • Right to object: because we do not process your data on any server or for any automated decision-making, there is no processing to object to.

Legal basis for processing: to the extent any processing occurs, it is based on your consent (which you provide by voluntarily entering data) and the legitimate interest of providing the App's core functionality. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data. Given the local-only nature of the App, we have not appointed a formal Data Protection Officer; for GDPR-related inquiries, contact us using the information in Section 13.

9.3 Other jurisdictions

We respect privacy rights under all applicable laws. If you are located in a jurisdiction with specific privacy rights not addressed above, please contact us using the information in Section 13, and we will respond in accordance with applicable law.

10. Apple App Tracking Transparency

Saturdays does not track you across other companies' apps or websites. We do not use the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) or any other tracking identifier. Because we do not engage in any tracking, the App does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt. Our App Store privacy nutrition label accurately reflects that we collect no data.

11. Security

While we do not transmit or store your data externally, we take reasonable measures to protect the data stored locally on your device through the App, including using iOS-native secure storage APIs, not logging or caching sensitive data in plaintext, and following Apple's recommended practices for local data storage. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to protect your device with a strong passcode and keep your iOS version up to date.
